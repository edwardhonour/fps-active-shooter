ISC BEST PRACTICES SUBCOMMITTEE ACTIVE SHOOTER “STAND-ALONE” PREPAREDNESS PLAN TEMPLATE OFFERING
PURPOSE: The purpose of this template is to assist departments and agencies in developing and/or improving policies, memorandums, or plans. This template contains information that provides a baseline for refinement at the organization or facility level.
BACKGROUND: The ISC Best Practices Subcommittee focuses on identifying best practices that can be generalized and have a high likelihood of success and transferability to other programs within or across the federal government. The Best Practices Subcommittee conducts in-depth interviews with program managers or process owners, reviews program or process documents, analyzes secondary sources, and compares program/process features and outcomes. This template is an ISC Best Practices Subcommittee product developed in consultation with ISC members on best practices, benchmarking information, and key lessons learned.
HOW TO CUSTOMIZE THIS TEMPLATE: Throughout this standalone active shooter plan, delete italicized labels or instructions and replace them with your facility’s information. Where applicable, fill empty table boxes with your facility information, adding or deleting rows as necessary. Mark any information not applicable to your facility with an N/A. This template can be augmented with any facility or organizational specific information necessary to ensure completeness. For questions or suggestions on this template, please email: ISC.DHS.GOV@HQ.DHS.GOV
